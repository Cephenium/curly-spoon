Our Mission

TEDxJSS International School Youth is an independently organized TED event that brings together young minds, innovative thinkers, and inspiring speakers to share ideas worth spreading. Our mission is to foster creativity, critical thinking, and positive change within our school community and beyond.

❓

What is TEDx?

TEDx events are local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event includes live speakers and TED Talks videos to spark deep discussion and connection.

🌱

Why Youth?

Young people have unique perspectives and innovative solutions to global challenges. Our youth-focused event amplifies these voices and encourages the next generation of leaders.
